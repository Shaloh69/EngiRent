## üîß ML Model Training Considerations

When training the YOLOv8 model for item verification:

### High-Priority Categories (Train First)
1. Lab Gowns - High demand, simple shape
2. Calculators - Distinct features, multiple brands
3. Power Banks - Various sizes/brands
4. Drawing Tools - Distinctive items

### Medium-Priority Categories
5. Laptops - Brand variations
6. Arduino/Raspberry Pi - Small but distinctive
7. PE Uniforms - Similar to lab gowns

### Low-Priority Categories (Train Later)
8. Headphones - Lower demand
9. Cameras - Lower demand, high value
10. Sports equipment - Lowest demand

### Training Dataset Requirements

For effective AI verification:
- **Minimum 100 images per category** (various angles)
- **Multiple brands per category** (e.g., CASIO, TI, HP for calculators)
- **Different lighting conditions**
- **Various backgrounds** (simulating kiosk environment)
- **Multiple item states** (new, used, with accessories)
